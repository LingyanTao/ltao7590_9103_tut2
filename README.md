## Part 2: Coding Technique Exploration  
I explored the technique of Perlin Noise motion combined with alpha crossfade in p5.js. Perlin Noise creates natural, organic movement suitable for simulating the fluid swimming of goldfish, similar to the opening sequence of *Sakuran*. Alpha crossfade enables smooth dissolve transitions between two moving scenes, allowing one goldfish to fade into another. Together, these techniques can recreate the flowing, dreamlike visual rhythm seen in the film.
